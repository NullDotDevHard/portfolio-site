About Joshua


Joshua is a music, video game, technology, and science enthusiast. He got into music at the age of 7, started playing video games at the age of 8, and built his first computer at 15. He started a band at 14 with his two friends. His interest in game development started when he was researching how to build a website with Html, and came accross game development tutorials for basic text adventures with Python. A few online tutorials later, and he applied to join the Boise State University College of Innovation and Design at the age of 17. Ever since then, he's been coming up with game ideas, and through this, his creativity skyrocketed. He then branched out into several other hobbies, creative writing, music composition, film scoring, and game design. He aspires to use his skills in software engineering, creative writing and music composition, to aid the fields of science, psychology, medical technology, social issues, discourse, and education. Joshua will graduate in Spring 2022, and will hopefully change the world shortly thereafter.
